Young Adult Recommnedations

In the mood for a heartwarming coming-of-age story that tackles important themes with sensitivity and humor? Look no further than 'The Hate U Give' by Angie Thomas. This powerful young adult novel follows sixteen-year-old Starr Carter as she grapples with identity, racism, and activism in the aftermath of a tragic shooting. With its authentic voice, compelling characters, and timely message, 'The Hate U Give' is a poignant and unforgettable read that will resonate with readers of all ages.

The Girl With The Dragon Tattoo

If you are a reader like me and you want and adventurous yet thrilling story to get into then this book is for you as it follows the story of a strong lady in the system and a journalist as they uncover years of corelated sexual assualt cases linked to the man who hired them. There is also a movie adaptation which is just as captivating.

Fiction Recommnedation

December 23, 2023 by Tamara

For fans of thought-provoking science fiction, 'Dune' by Frank Herbert is a timeless classic that deserves a spot on your reading list. Set in a distant future where humanity has spread across the galaxy, 'Dune' follows the epic journey of Paul Atreides as he navigates political intrigue, religious prophecy, and the harsh desert landscape of the planet Arrakis.Herbert's rich world-building, complex characters, and philosophical themes make 'Dune' a must-read for any sci-fi enthusiast
